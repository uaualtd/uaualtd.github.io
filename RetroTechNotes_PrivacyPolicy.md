# Retro Tech Notes – Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 22 February 2026_

Retro Tech Notes is designed from the ground up as a **privacy‑first, fully offline** note‑taking app. This policy explains what data we handle and, more importantly, what we **do not** collect or do.

---

## 1. Overview

- **No ads**
- **No analytics or tracking**
- **No accounts or logins**
- **No cloud or server‑side storage**
- **No data sharing with any third parties**

All of your notes and settings live **only on your device**. We, as the developer, never see or receive your content.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Specifically:

- We do **not** collect your name, email, or any contact details.
- We do **not** collect your notes, tags, attachments, or any content you create.
- We do **not** collect device identifiers, usage statistics, or analytics.
- We do **not** log your activity or track you in any way.

There is no in‑app account system, and there is no registration or sign‑in of any kind.

---

## 3. On‑Device Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device**:

- Your notes and related metadata are saved to on‑device storage.
- No automatic backups are sent to our servers (we do not operate any).
- If your device has system‑level backups (e.g., iCloud, iTunes, or similar), those backups are controlled by your operating system and your own settings, not by Retro Tech Notes.

If you uninstall the app, the locally stored notes and settings associated with the app may be removed from your device, depending on your platform’s behavior.

---

## 4. Encryption and Security

Retro Tech Notes uses encrypted local storage for sensitive data where supported by the platform.

While we take reasonable technical steps to store your data securely **on your device**, no system can be perfectly secure. You are responsible for:

- Keeping your device’s operating system up to date.
- Using a strong device passcode/biometric lock.
- Managing any system‑level backups (e.g., iCloud, Google Drive) according to your own privacy needs.

We never receive your encryption keys or have remote access to your device data.

---

## 5. Network Access and Connectivity

Retro Tech Notes is built to function **fully offline**:

- The core note‑taking experience does **not** require an internet connection.
- The app does **not** contact any developer‑controlled servers.
- The app does **not** perform background data transfers.

Any network connectivity is limited to system‑level services outside our control (for example, app store updates or operating system services).

---

## 6. Sharing and Exporting Your Notes

Retro Tech Notes may allow you to **manually** export or share notes (for example, via your device’s native share sheet or by generating files that you choose to send).

Important details:

- Sharing/export is always **initiated by you**.
- When you choose to share, your data is sent **only to the apps or services you select** (e.g., email, messaging, cloud storage).
- We do **not** see or intercept any content you share.
- We do **not** have any agreements to receive data from those third‑party apps or services.

You remain in full control of where your data goes when you choose to share it.

---

## 7. Third‑Party Services and SDKs

Retro Tech Notes:

- Does **not** integrate any third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- Does **not** send your data to external marketing, profiling, or advertising services.
- Does **not** sell, rent, or trade any data, because we do not collect it in the first place.

The only third parties involved are:

- **Platform stores** (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play) handling app distribution and payments.
- **System components** (e.g., OS share sheet, file pickers) that you explicitly use. These are governed by your device’s operating system and its own privacy policies.

We do not receive any personal data from these platform services beyond what is strictly necessary for app distribution (e.g., anonymous sales/download counts at an aggregate level, with no access to your notes or identity).

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Retro Tech Notes is a general‑audience productivity app and does not target children specifically.

Because we do **not** collect or store personal information on our servers, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child’s use of the app, you can delete the app and associated local data from the device at any time.

---

## 9. Your Control Over Your Data

Because all data resides on your device:

- You can delete individual notes from within the app.
- You can delete the entire dataset by uninstalling the app from your device (subject to your OS’s backup behavior).
- You can export or share your notes to your own storage or services of choice.

We cannot restore deleted data or access your notes — there is no server‑side copy.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will:

- Update this privacy policy accordingly.
- Update the “Last updated” date at the top.
- Ensure any new behavior continues to respect user privacy and, if needed, request additional consent in‑app.

We will not quietly start collecting personal data; any such change would be clearly disclosed and require a new version of the app.
