# Site Survey - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Site Survey is designed as a privacy-first, offline-first site surveying app. This policy explains what information Site Survey handles, what it does not collect, and how your data is stored.

## 1. Overview

Site Survey is built around local, on-device usage.

- No user accounts or sign-in.
- No advertising SDKs.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No developer-operated cloud backend for your survey data.

Your sites, checklists, photos, custom fields, templates, and report branding settings are stored on your device.

## 2. Data We Collect

Site Survey does not collect personal data directly through developer servers.

Specifically, Site Survey does not directly collect or transmit to the developer:

- Your name, email, phone number, or contact details.
- Your sites, checklist items, photos, notes, or custom field values.
- Your company or report branding details (such as company name, contact block, or logo).
- Your usage analytics or behavior tracking data.

## 3. On-Device Data Storage

Site Survey stores app data locally on your device using on-device app storage (a local SQLite database and the app's private file storage).

This includes, for example:

- Sites and their checklist items, photos, notes, tags, and custom field values.
- Photos captured from the camera or imported from your photo library, copied into app-private storage.
- Survey templates.
- Report branding and app preferences (such as company details, report author, logo, and display choices).
- Premium entitlement cache state used for purchase restoration behavior.

If you uninstall Site Survey, locally stored app data may be removed according to your platform's behavior.

## 4. Camera and Photo Library

Site Survey can capture photos using your device camera or import photos from your photo library, so you can attach them to a site and include them in reports.

- Photos are stored locally in the app's private storage on your device.
- Photos are only included in a PDF report when you choose to generate, share, or print one.
- Site Survey does not upload your photos to any developer server.

Your device will request camera and photo library permission before access is granted. You can change these permissions at any time in your device settings.

## 5. PDF Reports, Sharing, and Backups

Site Survey can generate PDF reports and create local backup files from your survey data.

- Reports and backups are created on your device.
- When you share, print, or save a report or backup, it is handled through your device's native share, print, or file system, under your control.
- You choose where exported files are sent or saved. Once you share a file with another app or service, that destination handles the file under its own terms.

## 6. In-App Purchases

Site Survey offers an optional Pro subscription (for example, unlimited sites, custom report branding, and backup/restore).

Purchases are processed by your platform store provider, such as:

- Apple App Store
- Google Play

Important details:

- Payment processing is handled by the store, not by the Site Survey developer.
- Site Survey may receive purchase status/entitlement signals from the store so features can be unlocked or restored.
- Site Survey does not receive your full payment card details.

Your transactions with Apple or Google are governed by their terms and privacy policies.

## 7. Network Access and Connectivity

The core Site Survey experience is designed to work fully offline with locally stored data.

Network activity may occur for limited platform-managed reasons, including:

- In-app purchase flows and restore checks through Apple/Google services.
- App distribution, updates, and store infrastructure operations.

Site Survey does not run a developer backend that receives your survey data.

## 8. Third-Party Services and SDKs

Site Survey uses essential platform and framework components to function (for example, the Flutter runtime and app store billing infrastructure).

Site Survey does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs for user tracking.

Where third-party platform services are involved (such as Apple/Google purchase services), those providers process data under their own policies.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Site Survey is a general-audience app and is not directed specifically to children.

Because Site Survey does not operate developer servers to collect personal data from users, the developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children through Site Survey.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, you can remove app data by deleting app data or uninstalling the app on the device.

## 10. Your Control Over Data

Because Site Survey data is stored locally on your device:

- You can edit or remove sites, photos, and other in-app data within the app.
- You can clear local app data using your device/app settings.
- You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored data (subject to device backup behavior).

## 11. Data Security

Site Survey uses reasonable technical measures provided by the platform and app framework for local data handling.

No software environment is perfectly secure. You are responsible for device-level security controls such as passcodes, biometrics, operating system updates, and backup settings.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

If Site Survey data practices change, this policy will be updated.

When updates happen, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised.
