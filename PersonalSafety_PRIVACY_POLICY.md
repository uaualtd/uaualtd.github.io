## Device Permissions Explained

To provide essential safety features, the App requires certain device permissions. Below is a transparent explanation of each permission, why it is needed, and how it is used:

### üìç Location Permissions

**Permissions:**
- `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` (Precise Location)
- `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` (Approximate Location)
- `ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION` (Background Location Access)

**Purpose:** To include your current location in emergency SOS messages and enable real-time location sharing with your designated emergency contacts.

**How It's Used:**
- When you activate an SOS alert, your location coordinates are included in the emergency message sent to your chosen contacts
- The Periodic Location Sharing feature sends your location to contacts you select at intervals you configure
- Background location access ensures the app can continue sharing your location even when the screen is off or the app is in the background

**Privacy Assurance:** Your location data is ONLY shared with contacts YOU explicitly choose. Location data is NEVER sent to our servers or any third parties. You have full control over when and with whom your location is shared.

---

### üì± SMS Permission

**Permission:** `SEND_SMS`

**Purpose:** To send emergency text messages directly from your device to your designated emergency contacts.

**How It's Used:**
- When you trigger an SOS alert, the app sends SMS messages containing your emergency information and location to your pre-selected contacts
- Uses your device's native SMS capability‚Äîmessages are sent as standard text messages from your phone number

**Privacy Assurance:** SMS messages are sent directly from your device using your phone's standard messaging service. We have no access to these messages.

---

### üìû Phone State Permission

**Permission:** `READ_PHONE_STATE`

**Purpose:** To detect the device state and ensure reliable SMS delivery during emergencies.

**How It's Used:**
- Verifies SMS service availability before attempting to send emergency messages
- Ensures the app can properly handle interruptions (such as incoming calls) during SOS activation

**Privacy Assurance:** This permission is used solely for technical reliability. We do not access, collect, or store any phone call data, phone numbers, or telephony information.

---

### üìá Contacts Permission

**Permission:** `READ_CONTACTS`

**Purpose:** To allow you to easily import emergency contacts from your device's address book.

**How It's Used:**
- When you choose to add emergency contacts, you can import them directly from your phone's contacts
- This is entirely optional‚Äîyou may also manually enter contact information

**Privacy Assurance:** Contact data is only accessed when you actively choose to import contacts. Your contacts list is NEVER uploaded, copied, or transmitted anywhere. Contact information selected for emergencies is stored locally on your device only.

---

### üì∏ Camera Permission

**Permission:** `CAMERA`

**Purpose:** To control your device's flashlight/torch for the emergency strobe light feature.

**How It's Used:**
- Enables the SOS strobe light feature that flashes your camera's LED to attract attention during emergencies
- **The camera is NOT used to capture photos or videos**‚Äîonly the flashlight component is accessed

**Privacy Assurance:** No photos or videos are ever taken. The camera hardware is accessed solely to control the flashlight LED.

---

### üì≥ Vibration Permission

**How It's Used:**
- Creates attention-getting vibration patterns when SOS is triggered
- Provides tactile confirmation of app actions

**Privacy Assurance:** This is a basic hardware control permission with no data implications.

### üîî Notification Permission

**Purpose:** To display important safety alerts and maintain foreground service notifications.

**How It's Used:**
- Shows persistent notifications when safety services (like location sharing or check-in timers) are active
- Displays check-in reminder alerts
- Shows fall detection alerts

**Privacy Assurance:** Notifications are generated and displayed locally on your device only.

### üåê Network Permissions

**Permissions:**
- `INTERNET`
- `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`

**Purpose:** To display map tiles showing your location and check network connectivity.

**How It's Used:**
- Downloads map imagery from standard map tile providers (OpenStreetMap) to show your location visually
- Checks if an internet connection is available to inform you if map features will work

**Privacy Assurance:** Network access is used only for displaying maps. We do not operate any servers that receive your data. No personal information is transmitted over the network.

---

### ‚è∞ Alarm Permission

**Permission:** `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`

**Purpose:** To ensure timely delivery of check-in reminders.

**How It's Used:**
- Powers the Timed Check-In feature that prompts you at set intervals
- Ensures alerts are delivered precisely when scheduled, even in battery-saving modes

**Privacy Assurance:** This is a system scheduling permission with no data collection implications.

---

### üì± Sensor Permissions

**Permissions:**
- `HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS`

**Purpose:** To enable fall detection using your device's accelerometer and gyroscope.

**How It's Used:**
- Monitors device motion patterns to detect potential falls
- If a fall is detected, triggers a countdown allowing you to cancel or automatically send an emergency alert

**Privacy Assurance:** Sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real-time. No sensor data is stored or transmitted anywhere.

---

### üîÑ Background & Boot Permissions

**Permissions:**
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE`
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION`
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC`
- `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_HEALTH`
- `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`

**Purpose:** To maintain safety services running reliably in the background and restore them after device restart.

**How It's Used:**
- Keeps location sharing, check-in timers, and fall detection active even when the app is in the background
- Automatically restarts active safety features after your device reboots

**Privacy Assurance:** These are technical permissions that enable the app to function as a reliable safety tool. They do not involve any data collection.

## Third-Party Services

The App displays maps using OpenStreetMap tile servers. When viewing maps, your device requests map images based on the geographic area displayed. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy governs their handling of these requests. No personal information is shared with map providers by our app.

The App offers optional premium features through Google Play Billing (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Google or Apple respectively. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information.
