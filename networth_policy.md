# NetWorth Tracker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

NetWorth Tracker is designed as a privacy-first personal finance app. This policy explains what information NetWorth Tracker handles, what it does not collect, and how your data is stored.

## 1. Overview

NetWorth Tracker is built around local, on-device usage.

- No user accounts or sign-in.
- No advertising SDKs.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No developer-operated cloud backend for your financial data.

Your accounts, balances, assets, liabilities, and transactions are stored on your device.

## 2. Data We Collect

NetWorth Tracker does not collect personal data directly through developer servers.

Specifically, NetWorth Tracker does not directly collect or transmit to the developer:

- Your name, email, phone number, or contact details.
- Your accounts, balances, assets, liabilities, or transaction history.
- Your net worth or cashflow figures.
- Your usage analytics or behavior tracking data.

## 3. On-Device Data Storage

NetWorth Tracker stores app data locally on your device using on-device app storage.

This includes, for example:

- Account, asset, liability, and transaction data you enter.
- Net worth and cashflow calculations derived from your entries.
- App preferences (such as display, currency, and customization choices).

If you uninstall NetWorth Tracker, locally stored app data may be removed according to your platform's behavior.

## 4. Data Export and Import

NetWorth Tracker lets you export your data to a backup file and import it back.

Important details:

- Export and import are initiated by you and use your device's file system or file picker.
- Exported backup files are stored where you choose to save them and are under your control.
- The developer does not receive a copy of your exported data.
- You are responsible for the security of any backup files you create, share, or store elsewhere.

## 5. Network Access and Connectivity

The core NetWorth Tracker experience is designed to work entirely with locally stored data.

Network activity may occur only for limited platform-managed reasons, including:

- App distribution, updates, and store infrastructure operations.

NetWorth Tracker does not run a developer backend that receives your account or transaction data.

## 6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

NetWorth Tracker uses essential platform and framework components to function (for example, the Flutter runtime and platform storage APIs).

NetWorth Tracker does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs for user tracking.

Where platform services are involved (such as app store distribution), those providers process data under their own policies.

## 7. Children's Privacy

NetWorth Tracker is a general-audience app and is not directed specifically to children.

Because NetWorth Tracker does not operate developer servers to collect personal data from users, the developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children through NetWorth Tracker.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, you can remove app data by deleting app data or uninstalling the app on the device.

## 8. Your Control Over Data

Because NetWorth Tracker data is stored locally on your device:

- You can edit or remove your data within the app.
- You can export your data to a backup file at any time.
- You can clear local app data using your device/app settings.
- You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored data (subject to device backup behavior).

## 9. Data Security

NetWorth Tracker uses reasonable technical measures provided by the platform and app framework for local data handling.

No software environment is perfectly secure. You are responsible for device-level security controls such as passcodes, biometrics, operating system updates, and backup settings.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

If NetWorth Tracker data practices change, this policy will be updated.

When updates happen, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised.
